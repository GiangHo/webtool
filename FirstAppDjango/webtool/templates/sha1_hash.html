About SHA-1

What is SHA-1?

SHA-1 stands for Secure Hash Algorithm 1. It is a cryptographic hash algorithm. SHA-1 is developed by National Security Agency and published by National Institute of Standards and Technology. SHA-1 is used in checksums for a file.

Length of SHA-1 is 160 bits.
